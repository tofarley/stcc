About This Site

What Is This?

This is an unofficial solo bot companion for Star Trek: Captain's Chair, a deckbuilding card game. Instead of needing a second player, you play your normal game while this site runs an automated opponent bot for you — flipping cards, issuing actions, and managing its own deck according to each Bot Captain's unique behavior.

How to Play

  1. Choose a Bot Captain from the home screen. Higher difficulty captains have more aggressive or complex behaviors.
  2. On the bot's turn, enter how many cards to flip based on the current stardate card and click Flip Cards.
  3. Each card shows the bot's action for that card type. Follow the on-screen instructions — discard cards, log them out of the game, deploy ships, promote Duty Officers, and so on — using the provided buttons.
  4. Move any physical components as needed to reflect the game state (primarily ships and away teams at neutral locations).
  5. When you're done resolving the turn, click End Turn to discard the drawn cards and prepare for the next turn.
  6. Use the sidebar to track the bot's resources, Duty Officer, deployed cards, and deck counts. You can also browse the bot's discard and log piles at any time.

Tech Stack

The site is built with Python and Flask on the backend, with HTMX handling dynamic page updates without writing JavaScript. Game state is stored entirely in your browser session — there is no database.

Privacy

Your game state is stored in a session cookie in your own browser. Nothing is saved on any server. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you — ever. Closing your browser or clearing cookies ends your session.

Playtest Version — This site is under active development. You may encounter bugs, including ones that prevent you from completing a game. Thanks for helping test!
